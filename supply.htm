Where We Get Our Wood

With camps located around the world most of our high quality wood comes from local farmers in, North America, Africa, South America, and Europe. The wood then gets shipped to building facilities in Canada, Ontario, where they are cleaned, processed and finalized. In a facility down the road the furniture and products are then built.
